## Human verification checklist (Task 2)

Run: `cd apps/ptrack` then `fvm flutter run`.

**Calendar**

1. Tap an empty past day → day detail shows “I had my period”. Tap it → day marked; band shows that day.
2. Tap the day before the marked day → “I had my period” → period extends to two days.
3. Tap the marked day → “Add symptoms”, “Remove this day”, “Delete entire period”.
4. Tap “Add symptoms” → symptom form (flow/pain/mood/notes only). Save.
5. Tap the day with symptoms → data + “Edit”, “Clear symptoms”, “Remove this day”.
6. Tap “Remove this day” on a middle day of a 3+ day period → period splits into two.
7. Tap a future day → view-only, no action buttons.
8. If predictions show: future predicted day → prediction card, no actions.
9. Past predicted day → prediction card + “I had my period”.
